Tiptoe Soles: Born on the Northern Beaches

Tiptoe Soles was conceived in 2003, not in a factory, but in a small, sun-drenched shed overlooking Manly Beach. Liam O’Connell, a former lifeguard and self-taught leatherworker, was tired of uncomfortable, imported shoes that couldn't handle the harsh Australian sun and endless coastal tracks. His vision was to craft footwear that embodied the nation's relaxed, durable spirit.

Liam sourced tough, breathable canvas and pioneered the use of recycled materials, designing a shoe specifically for the Australian foot—supportive enough for a bushwalk, but cool enough for a Sydney cafe. The brand quickly grew by word-of-mouth, appealing to locals who valued quality, comfort, and an authentic, homegrown aesthetic. Today, Tiptoe Soles remains committed to its roots, designing every pair in Sydney to be the perfect, laid-back companion for the Australian market.

The Workshop: Crafting Soles in Sydney’s Inner West

After years in the humble Manly shed, Liam O’Connell knew Tiptoe Soles needed a proper home, but one that stayed true to their coastal values. In 2010, the company moved production to a repurposed warehouse in the inner west suburb of Marrickville—a hub known for its creative industries and industrial past.

The "factory," affectionately nicknamed 'The Workshop,' is less of a cold industrial space and more of a sunlit collective. It features custom-built, low-energy machinery and uses rainwater harvesting to minimize its footprint. Every stitch is handled by a small team of master craftspeople, many of whom are lifelong Sydney locals. They oversee the careful integration of recycled rubber soles and organic canvas, ensuring each shoe meets Liam’s original standard for Australian durability and comfort. The Marrickville Workshop is the beating heart of Tiptoe Soles, proving that world-class, ethical manufacturing can thrive right here in Sydney.

Core Values of Tiptoe Soles

Tiptoe Soles operates on three foundational principles, stemming directly from Liam O’Connell’s original Manly shed vision.

Firstly, Authentic Australian Craftsmanship guides everything we do. We are committed to Sydney, utilizing our small team of local master craftspeople in The Workshop to ensure every stitch embodies dedication and quality that only local manufacturing can provide.

Secondly, Radical Sustainability is non-negotiable. We believe in building shoes, not waste, pioneering the use of recycled rubber and organic canvas. We employ low-energy machinery and utilize rainwater harvesting to minimize our environmental footprint.

Finally, we prioritize Uncompromising Comfort and Durability. Our shoes are engineered specifically for the varied Australian lifestyle—tough enough for a bushwalk, yet comfortable and cool enough to be the reliable, laid-back companion for every adventure and cafe visit.
